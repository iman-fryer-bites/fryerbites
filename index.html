Air Fryer Brownies

Rich, fudgy brownies made quickly in your air fryer. Perfect for late-night cravings or quick desserts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth
  2. Pour into a greased pan
  3. Air fry at 180°C for 12 minutes
